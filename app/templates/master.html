Capstone project:  Heart Failure Prediction

Project Motivation - Defintion

"Heart disease has remained the leading cause of death at the global level for the last 20 years. However, it is now killing more people than ever before" (WHO).


Although the risk of a cardiovascular disease can be greatly reduced by a healthy lifestyle, there are still many people who are not sufficiently aware of the dangers or simply cannot avoid them due to their poor living conditions. A verly large group of people could therefore benefit from a machine learning algorithm that would predict, based on a number of objectively measurable social and medical criteria , that the event of a fatal hart failure is imminent.

The aim of this project was therefore to build a machine learning model based on a dataset with a number of medical and social conditions that would allow us to make a prediction for a hart failure and which are the most important features that influence this prediction. One can try it by selecting "Make a prediction" on the navigation bar.
